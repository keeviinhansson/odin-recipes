Carbonara Recipe

The classical spaghetti carbonara!

Become the italian you strive to be in the kitchen!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti
  2. Fry the pancetta
  3. Mix an egg and the cheese until emulsified
  4. Turn off the heat on the pancetta
  5. Drain spaghetti, add it and the egg/cheese mix to the pancetta and mix it together
