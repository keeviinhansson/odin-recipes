Tacos Recipe

The most perfect hardshell tacos!

Way to easy to make, way too good!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or vegetarian option
  • Taco seasoning
  • Lettuce, corn, cucumber, tomatoes
  • Hardshell tacos or tortillas
  • Salsa

Recipe instructions

  1. Dice lettuce, cucumber and tomatoes
  2. Fry the beef and add the taco seasoning
  3. Heat the hardshell in oven for couple of minutes, or microwave tortillas for 1 minute.
  4. Assemble tacos!
