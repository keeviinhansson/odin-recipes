Lasagna Recipe

A perfect piece of homemade Lasagna!

Perfect goto meal prep! Tasty and easy as ever!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or vegetarian option
  • Onion
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Lasagna pasta plates
  • Parmesan

Recipe instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 175 celsius
  2. Finely dice onion. Fry ground beef, onion in a frying pan.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and let simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Alternate between adding sauce, beef and pasta plates into an oven dish
  5. Cook in the middle of the oven for 40 minutes
